Separating the power like this is important as the current draw by the motors could impact the performance of the Arduino. If both the motors and the Arduino used the same power supply you would see sporadic bugs and difficult to trace issues with your code, so you would think. So remember: Separation of Powers.<br /><br />The switch is used to turn on and off the power to the motors.
